How to use War Room

Home

War Room is a web application designed to make everyone capable of harnessing the most lucrative and prolific resource of the modern era - Data. Just being computer literate is enough to make impactful visualizations. Here's how-

1 - Find a suitable data point


War Room will not let you scrape every point on the web, whether it be blocked, behind some sort of wall we can't simulate like authentication/authorization or captcha, or if it's not allowed by War Room itself. However, more often than not, all you need is a number or string you want.

2 - Point War Room at a target


There are 2 necessary elements you need to get from any page you want data from. The URL (i.e. google.com) and the X-Path. The X-Path is a selector that says where the data is in context of the page. Here's how we would get the temperature from weather.com:

Highlight the data, right click, inspect, and on the corresponding element in the inspector copy > copy X Path.
